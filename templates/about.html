Core Technologies

Our proxy-proof system relies on three robust technological pillars to ensure total accuracy.

Geofencing

Utilizes HTML5 Geolocation API and the Haversine formula to ensure students are physically within 50 meters of the teacher's coordinates before the server accepts their scan.

Timed Tokens

QR codes are securely encrypted with UUIDs and automatically expire after exactly 3 minutes. This makes it impossible to share active links with absent friends later.

MySQL Security

Direct integration with Django's ORM and a robust MySQL backend for secure data storage. Includes duplicate-entry prevention for robust attendance logging.
